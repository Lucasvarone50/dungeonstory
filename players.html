Randy

Dwarf, Battlemaster Fighter

Bio:

Hailing from the northern hills Randy is a headstrong hill dwarf skilled in crafting of tools and the art of brewing. Randy always returns to the hills with numerous treasures and artifacts to share. Randy's Origin is blurry. He has lived in the hills for over 30 years, but his past is a mystery. Whenever asked. Randy always says something like. "The past is the past" and changes subjects.

Fighting Style:

Randy is a skilled fighter that fights with an axe in one hand and a shield in the other. He always enters a fight with full defenses up, which makes him next to impossible to touch. Though he is a close combat fighter, he is not affraid to chuck an axe at his opponent from a distance.
