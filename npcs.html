Eldras is the local blacksmith in Red Larch. He is so busy with his work that that he doesnt pay attention to whats going on in town. He sells basic weapons and armor.

Endrith is the owner of Vallivoe Sundries, the local shop in Red Larch for miscellaneous items. His shop is crammed with items, some useless but some of value. He is alwasy looking to buy and sell.

Shagy

Local drug dealer that sells the finest herbs. He is very paranoid and likes to hang out in the market area of Red Larch. Does not like to bargain.

Helvur Tarnlar

Owner and seemstress of Tarnlar's Clothier. Her shop is the only place in Red Larch that crafts and sells fine garmets for fashion and practicality. She is always dressed in fine clothes. Her shop is not for those without money to spend.

Savra Helabranta

A Noble Woman from Waterdeep that left her home in seek of adventure. Not wanteing the life of a noble woman, Savra chose to train in combat. She ended up joining the Feathergale Society and her loyalty to them seems absolute.

Thurl Merosska

The commander of the Feathergale Knights is a well-built male human in his early fifties. Embossed feather patterns ornament the plates of his armor, and his kingly cloak boasts a feathered mantle. He smooths his white-blond hair into place, then bows low before you as if he were only a lowly courtier.

Lady Ushien Stormbanner

Lady Ushien Stormbanner is a decorated knight of Waterdeep who has served as the leader of the noble knights of Samular at Summit Hall for almost 20 years. Now in her forties, Lady Stormbanner is still hale, hearty and competitive. The other Knights of Samular are older, aging relics of an earlier age. Still, novice fighters still come to train with them.
