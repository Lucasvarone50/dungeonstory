ITEMS

Drift Globe

Globe sheds bright in a 60’ radius, dim light in 120’ radius. Stays a handspan above owner unless otherwise commanded.

Sword of the Sun

Longsword. Shines with a faint gold glow during the day. Shines bright light in 60' radius when command word spoken.

Ring of Featherfall

Wearer can cast Featherfall on themselves freely.

Wand of Magic Missile

Casts Magic Missile. Has 8 charges that can be replenished by rolling d6 each morning.

Cloak of Protection

Gives the wearer +1 AC. This cloak has the Cult of the Black Earth symbol.

Wind Scimitar

When attacking, a blade of wind shoots from the sword that goes up to 30ft.

Axe of the Moon

When the moon is in the sky the axe has a black aura and becomes a hand axe +1

Reszur

+1 Dagger. Has the name Reszur engraved in it.

Bag of Holding

A bag that opens into another dimension. Can hold up to 500 pounds, not exceeding a volume of 64 cubic feet.
