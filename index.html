PRINCES OF THE APOCALYPSE

STORY

A terrible threat gathers in the North. Throughout the Dessarian Valley savage marauders bring destruction. Monsters are on the prowl, preying on flocks, rampaging through croplands, and attacking homesteads and travelers. In settlements, discord and suspicion grow. Sinister strangers lurk in the shadows, whispering about how everything is soon to change. Rumours of Elemental Evil has surfaced in the world. Talk is spreading fast of the return of certain evil elemental cults that seek to master elemental magic and unleash its destructive power everywhere.
